MRA - Music identifier - Music Recognition

MRA (short for Music Recognition Application) is a music recognition application, or music identifier, like Shazam but for PC.
MRA listens to your microphone or the desktop sound and identifies the music
and then gives you information about the music you found like title, artist, album, genre, etc. You can listen to the music on different platforms, see lyrics, see similar kinds of music, and much more.
